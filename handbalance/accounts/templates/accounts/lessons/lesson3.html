Lesson 3

The first lesson will dedicated to basic exercises, which preparing your body for learning handstand. Lets go!


Exercise 1

Original position lying on the floor. Your body must be maximum straight. We should lie down straight and forming a right line. small of the back must be pinned to the floor as shown in the video. Hands should touch the floor (if pressing requires effort, do the shoulder stretch as shown in the insert)
FIXATION ABOUT 1 MIN

Exercise 2

Imitation handstand with chair Firtly put our feet on a chair We approach the chair with our hands so that the buttockswere at the top If your flexibility dont allows you keep your knees straight you can SLIGHTLY bend it. On this moment we show in what position your soulders should NOT TO BE As you can se Im falling down on my shoulders. Its a big mistake The shoulders should be pulled in as much as possible and pressed against the ears as you can seen when viewed from the side. This shows the correct position of your brushes. As you can see they are looking straight. Toes are spread out to the sides as much as possible and bent. It will be handy for your balance. No need to close your fingers as shown in the video with the wall.
FIXATION 2*60 SEC

Exercise 3

Next step. Handstand with the wall The video where I stand shows how it should and should not be done. HOW TO DO? Stretch your feet and knees by straining your quadriceps so you can feel your legs more easily. Straining your glutes is your center of gravity in handstand. slightly retract the abdomen and squeeze the chest inward. Pull your shoulders up as we did in the second exercise. THE BODY SHOULD REMIND A Smooth LINE. HOW NOT TO DO IT SHOWN ON THE VIDEO AS WELL.



The theory is finished Lets practice now We approach the wall lifting one leg then the other Here we find ourselves in a obsidedown position We take a couple of steps this time with our hands Approaching as close as possible with your hands to the wall Now we form a straight line as we did in the previous exercise. We tighten the buttocks slightly retract the stomach and squeeze the chest Pull soulders up The position of the elbows is shown here, they should be as straight as possible. No need to bend them like in the video To get off, we lower one leg to the side and then the other So BANG and we are on our feet At this stage, I advise you to shoot yourself on the camera to see mistakes.
